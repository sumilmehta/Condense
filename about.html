What we do?


We aim to deliver the best compression techniques to all our consumers and make their user experience as smooth as possible.Condense is a compression tool which works on the DEFLATE algorithm. The DEFLATE algorithm is a combination of Huffman Algorithm and LZ77 algorithm.
